# 🚦 Road Accident Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Problem Statement

Road accidents are a major public safety concern. This project analyzes accident data to identify patterns related to location, time, vehicle type, and severity in order to support data-driven decision-making for reducing accidents.

---

## 📁 Dataset Overview

* Contains accident records including:

  * Date & Time of accident
  * Location (Urban/Rural)
  * Vehicle type
  * Casualties & severity
* Data cleaned and transformed using SQL and Power BI

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

* Total Accidents
* Total Casualties
* Fatal vs Non-Fatal Accidents
* Accident Severity Distribution
* Monthly Trend Analysis
* Urban vs Rural Accident Ratio

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Majority of accidents occur in **urban areas**, indicating high traffic density risk
* **Evening hours** show peak accident frequency due to rush hours
* **Two-wheelers and cars** contribute to the highest accident share
* A significant gap between accidents and fatalities highlights scope for better emergency response
* Seasonal trends indicate higher accidents during specific months

---

## 🧠 Business Impact

* Helps authorities identify **high-risk zones**
* Supports better **traffic management strategies**
* Assists in improving **road safety policies**
* Enables targeted awareness campaigns
